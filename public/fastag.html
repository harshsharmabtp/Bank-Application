Overview

IDBI FASTag is a thick paper-based tag/device that uses Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) technology for making toll payments directly from the Prepaid Wallet, Savings, or Current account linked to it. It is to be affixed on the windscreen of your vehicle, which enables you to drive through toll plazas without stopping for cash payments as toll charges.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is FASTag?

FASTag is a prepaid tag that uses RFID technology for cashless toll payments on highways.

How do I get a FASTag?

You can obtain a FASTag from banks or authorized agents and link it to your bank account.

How does FASTag work?

The tag is affixed to your vehicle's windshield, allowing automatic toll deduction while passing through toll plazas.

Is there any fee for FASTag?

There is a one-time fee for the tag and a refundable deposit depending on the type of vehicle.

Can I use FASTag in multiple states?

Yes, FASTag is interoperable and can be used across all toll plazas in India.
