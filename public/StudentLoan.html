FAQ

What is the eligibility criteria?

You must be enrolled in a recognized institution.

How long does the approval process take?

It typically takes 3-5 business days for the approval process.

Can I repay the loan early?

Yes, there is no prepayment penalty. You can repay the loan early without any additional charges.

Is there a maximum loan amount?

Yes, you can borrow up to $50,000 based on your eligibility.

What is the interest rate?

The interest rate starts at 4.5%, depending on your credit score and loan amount.

When do I need to start repayment?

Repayment starts after graduation or after you are no longer enrolled in the institution.
